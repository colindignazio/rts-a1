Press the blue button for a short duration to cycle through the four drink types.
Press and hold the blue button for longer than a second to begin brewing the selected drink type.
While the drink is brewing the user may press the blue button for a short duration to reset the brew timer.
The user may also double click the blue button while the drink is brewing to return to the drink selection menu.
Once the drink is done brewing an alert will play through the 3.5mm audio port of the board and the user will be returned
to the drink selection menu.
